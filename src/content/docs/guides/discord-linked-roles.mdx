# Discord Linked Roles

Discord **Linked Roles** let server admins assign roles automatically when a member connects their TrucklineMP account through Discord. Members sign in on TrucklineMP once; Discord keeps their role connection up to date.

This works on **any** Discord server — community hubs, friend groups, event servers, and more. It is separate from VTC-specific features like server verification on your VTC profile.

---

## What You Get

- **Automatic verification** — Members prove they have a TrucklineMP account without manual role assignment
- **Staff detection** — Create a role that only TrucklineMP staff receive
- **Bot commands** — Linked members can use `/profile` to look up TrucklineMP profiles in Discord
- **Always in sync** — When a member's TrucklineMP status changes, their Linked Role connection updates on the next sync

---

## Requirements

### Server admins

- **Manage Server** permission in Discord
- The [TrucklineMP bot](https://discord.com/discovery/applications/1467506804727746854) added to your server (recommended for slash commands like `/profile`)

### Members

- A TrucklineMP account at [trucklinemp.com](https://trucklinemp.com)
- The Discord account they want to link must match the one they use in the connection flow

---

## Step 1: Add the TrucklineMP Bot

1. Open the TrucklineMP bot on Discord: [TrucklineMP on Discord](https://discord.com/discovery/applications/1467506804727746854)
2. Click **Add to Server** (or use the invite link from your VTC settings if you are verifying a VTC server)
3. Select your server and approve the requested permissions
4. Complete the installation

**Recommended bot permissions:**

- Read Messages / View Channels
- Send Messages
- Use Slash Commands

---

## Step 2: Create Linked Roles (Server Admins)

1. Open your Discord server
2. Go to **Server Settings**
3. Select **Linked Roles** in the sidebar
4. Click **Create Linked Role** (or **Connections** → **TrucklineMP** depending on your Discord client)
5. Choose the **TrucklineMP** application
6. Pick an existing role or create a new one to assign
7. Add a requirement using one of the TrucklineMP fields below
8. Save the Linked Role

Repeat for each role you want to gate (for example, a general **Member** role and a separate **Staff** role).

### Available TrucklineMP requirements

| Requirement | What it checks | Typical use |
|-------------|----------------|-------------|
| **Account linked** | Member has connected their TrucklineMP account | Verified member, general access, recruitment channels |
| **TrucklineMP staff** | Member is TrucklineMP platform staff | Staff-only channels, moderation areas |

When configuring the requirement in Discord, set **Account linked** to **Yes** (or equivalent) for verified members. For staff-only roles, add **TrucklineMP staff** set to **Yes**.

### Example setups

**Verified member role**

- Role: `@Member` (or your community's verified role)
- Requirement: **Account linked** = Yes

**Platform staff role**

- Role: `@TrucklineMP Staff`
- Requirement: **TrucklineMP staff** = Yes

You can combine both requirements on a single role if you only want staff who have also linked their account (staff always have a linked account in practice).

---

## Step 3: Members Connect Their Account

Each member completes this once per Discord account:

1. Open **User Settings** in Discord (click the gear next to your username)
2. Go to **Linked Roles** (under **Connections** on some clients)
3. Find **TrucklineMP** and click **Connect**
4. Sign in to TrucklineMP if prompted
5. Approve the connection
6. Discord returns you to the app; eligible Linked Roles are applied automatically

Members do **not** need to use **Settings → Connections** on TrucklineMP for Linked Roles. That page is for account recovery and profile linking — Linked Roles use Discord's dedicated connection flow.

---

## Bot Commands

After the bot is in your server and members have linked:

| Command | Who can use it | What it does |
|---------|----------------|--------------|
| `/profile [user]` | Everyone | Shows a linked member's TrucklineMP profile (name, handle, member since, Steam status) |
| `/about` | Everyone | Information about TrucklineMP |

VTC owners can additionally use `/verify-server` and `/vtc` after [verifying their server on TrucklineMP](/guides/vtc-discord-verification/).

If `/profile` says a user has not linked their account, they still need to complete **Step 3** above.

---

## Linked Roles vs. TrucklineMP Connections

These are two different features:

| | **Discord Linked Roles** | **TrucklineMP Connections** (Settings) |
|---|--------------------------|----------------------------------------|
| **Where** | Discord → User Settings → Linked Roles | trucklinemp.com → Settings → Connections |
| **Purpose** | Unlock Discord roles on a server | Account recovery, identity on your TrucklineMP profile |
| **Required for roles** | Yes | No |

Connecting Discord under TrucklineMP **Connections** does not by itself assign Linked Roles. Members must still connect through Discord's **Linked Roles** menu.

## Troubleshooting

### "TrucklineMP doesn't appear in Linked Roles"

- Confirm the TrucklineMP bot is in your server
- Make sure you are a server admin with **Manage Server**
- Try refreshing Server Settings or restarting the Discord client

### "Roles aren't assigned after connecting"

- Check that the Linked Role requirement matches what the member qualifies for (e.g. **Account linked** = Yes)
- Ensure the bot's role is **above** the roles it should assign in **Server Settings → Roles**
- Ask the member to disconnect and reconnect TrucklineMP under **User Settings → Linked Roles**

### "This Discord account does not match your linked TrucklineMP account"

- The member signed into TrucklineMP with an account already tied to a different Discord user
- They should use the same Discord account everywhere, or update their TrucklineMP connections first

### `/profile` shows "has not linked their TrucklineMP account"

- The member has not completed the Linked Roles connection flow
- Direct them to **User Settings → Linked Roles → TrucklineMP → Connect**

### Staff role not updating

- Staff status is read from TrucklineMP's staff system
- After a staff change on the website, the member's connection syncs automatically; they can reconnect in Linked Roles if needed

## Best Practices

- Create one **verified member** Linked Role before opening member-only channels
- Place the bot role high enough in the role list to assign your linked roles
- Pin a short message in `#rules` or `#welcome` explaining how to connect via **Linked Roles**
