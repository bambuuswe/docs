# VTC General Settings

The General Settings page is where you configure your VTC's core identity and public presence.

## Accessing General Settings

1. Navigate to your VTC's management panel
2. Select **General Settings** from the sidebar
3. You'll see sections for Identity, Requirements, Social Links, and Discord Server

---

## VTC Identity

### VTC Name
- Your VTC's display name visible throughout the platform
- Required field
- Changes affect how your VTC appears in directories and profiles

### VTC Handle
- Unique identifier for your VTC's URL: `trucklinemp.com/vtc/@yourhandle`
- Maximum 15 characters
- Only lowercase letters, numbers, and underscores allowed
- Displayed with an `@` prefix in the interface
- Changes your VTC's permanent URL

### Slogan
- Short tagline displayed on your VTC profile
- Maximum 60 characters
- Appears below your VTC name on the profile header

### Description
- Rich text description of your VTC (supports Markdown)
- Maximum 5,000 characters
- Required field
- Displayed on your VTC's public profile
- Use this to tell your VTC's story, mission, and what makes you unique

### Rules
- Your VTC's rules and guidelines (supports Markdown)
- Required field
- Visible to members and applicants
- Keep these clear and comprehensive

### Logo
- Profile picture URL for your VTC
- Must be a valid HTTPS URL
- Displayed in directories, profiles, and news feeds
- Recommended: Square image, at least 256x256px

### Banner
- Cover banner URL for your VTC profile
- Must be a valid HTTPS URL
- Displayed at the top of your VTC's profile page
- Recommended: Wide image, at least 1920x400px

## Requirements & Recruitment

Configure who can join your VTC and how recruitment works.

### Minimum Account Age
- Set the minimum age (in days) for a TrucklineMP account to apply
- Helps filter out brand new accounts
- Set to 0 to allow any account age

### Minimum Playtime
- Set the minimum playtime (in hours) required to apply
- Ensures applicants have experience with the game
- Set to 0 to allow any playtime

### Primary Language
- Your VTC's primary language
- Options: English, German, French, Spanish, Polish
- Displayed on your VTC profile
- Helps users find VTCs that match their language

### Open for Recruitment
- Toggle to enable or disable recruitment
- When enabled, your VTC shows as "Hiring" in directories
- When disabled, your VTC shows as "Closed"
- Does not affect current members

## Social & Presence

Add links to your VTC's online presence. All URLs must be valid `https://` links.

### Discord Invite
- Link to your VTC's Discord server
- Use a permanent invite link if possible
- Displayed on your VTC profile

### Website URL
- Your VTC's official website
- Displayed on your VTC profile
- Must be a valid HTTPS URL

### Public Email
- Contact email for your VTC
- Displayed publicly on your profile
- Must be a valid email address

### Instagram
- Link to your VTC's Instagram profile
- Must be a valid HTTPS URL

### Twitter/X
- Link to your VTC's Twitter or X profile
- Must be a valid HTTPS URL

### Facebook
- Link to your VTC's Facebook page
- Must be a valid HTTPS URL

---

## Saving Changes

- Click **Save Configuration** at the bottom right to apply changes
- A floating save button appears as you scroll
- Changes take effect immediately
- You'll see a success toast notification when saved

---

## Best Practices

### Profile Completion
- Fill out all fields for a professional appearance
- Use high-quality images for logo and banner
- Write a compelling description that tells your story
- Keep rules clear and comprehensive

### URLs
- Use permanent links where possible
- Test all links after saving
- Use HTTPS URLs only

### Recruitment Settings
- Set reasonable requirements to filter applicants
- Keep recruitment open when actively looking for members
- Update language to match your community

---

## Troubleshooting

### "I can't save my changes"
- Ensure all required fields are filled (name, description, rules)
- Check that all URLs are valid HTTPS links
- Verify email format is correct

### "My images aren't showing"
- Ensure URLs are publicly accessible
- Use direct image links (not page links)
- Check that URLs start with `https://`

### "I want to change my VTC handle"
- Handles can be changed in General Settings
- This changes your VTC's URL
- Old URLs will not redirect
